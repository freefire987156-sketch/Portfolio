Who I Am

Hi! I'm Farhan, a 19-year-old creative from Delhi, India. I work at the intersection of visual arts and technology — bringing ideas to life through photo editing, video production, 3D design, and code.

On the creative side, I work with Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects, CorelDRAW, and Autodesk 3ds Max to craft stunning visuals and cinematic edits.
